About Us

Welcome To Ceylon Holidays

 For over 4 decades, Ceylon Holidays travel agency has been the trusted travel partner for Sri Lankans.Ceylon Holidays is an IATA approved outbound travel agency founded in 1995, making us one of the oldest IATA approved agencies in Sri Lanka. We are one of the most reputed outbound travel agents in Sri Lanka due to the commitment and dedication of our team of experts that are ready to serve your travel requirements 24 x 7. We will be there with you every step of the way from helping you plan your trip to organizing tours, arranging accommodation & transport and providing you with the best of travel offers globally! For all your travel needs be it tours, vacations, packages and flights choose Ceylon Holidays travel agency, Your Trusted Travel Partner!
